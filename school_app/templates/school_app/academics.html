Curriculum

Our school follows the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and 8-4-4 system, ensuring our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.

  • Early Years Education (PP1-PP2)
  • Lower Primary (Grade 1-3)
  • Upper Primary (Grade 4-6)
  • Junior Secondary (Grade 7-9)
  • Senior Secondary (Grade 10-12)

Academic Programs

We offer a variety of academic programs to cater to the diverse needs and talents of our students:

Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Sciences, Humanities, and Languages

Specialized Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics program with advanced labs and projects

Music, Drama, Visual Arts, and Creative Writing programs
